Want to keep track of your period and nothing more?

Getting tired of sketchy apps that try to appease you with glaring pink aesthetics and automatic calculations of when your next period might be due? Just want to have an easy-to-use calendar so that you'd have something to show to your gynecologist, but can't seem to find any pocket-sized calendars for that? Then this site's just for you.

You can easily use this webiste both on your computer and your phone. You can request for your data to be compiled into a list and download it as a .txt, .docx, .pdf, .mobi or .html file.

  • This site collects only the information that you give. This site uses your information only for (compilations), and nothing more. Your information is not being sold to third party members for ads.
